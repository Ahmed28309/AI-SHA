## CONTINUOUS ASSESSMENT SYSTEM

**CA (Continuous Assessment) - formerly AMS:**
- NOT restricted to Fridays - testing occurs throughout the week
- Real-time monitoring of "Learning Points" mastery
- Point-by-Point delivery ensures concept mastery before progression
- Continuous monitoring prevents knowledge gaps

**Assessment Hierarchy:**
- CAs: Daily/weekly assessments of specific learning points
- Periodics: Weekly comprehensive tests covering recent material
- Prefinals: Practice examinations before finals
- Finals: End-of-term comprehensive examinations
